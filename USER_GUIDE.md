# Homestead Management System - User Guide

Welcome to the Homestead Management System! This guide will help you get started, whether you're a traveler looking for a unique getaway or a host wanting to list your property.

## For Travelers

As a traveler, you can explore unique homestays, view property details, and book your next vacation.

### 1. Creating an Account

-   **Sign Up:** Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in your name, email, and password, and make sure to select the "Traveler" role.
-   **Login:** Once registered, you can log in using your email and password.

### 2. Exploring and Booking Properties

-   **Explore:** Navigate to the "Explore" page to see all available properties. You can use the search and filter options to find the perfect homestay based on location, price, and amenities.
-   **View Details:** Click on any property to see more details, including a full description, photos, and reviews from other travelers.
-   **Book a Stay:** On the property details page, select your desired dates in the booking calendar and click "Book Now". You will be prompted to confirm your booking details.

### 3. Managing Your Bookings

-   **My Bookings:** After logging in, you can access the "My Bookings" page from the navigation bar. Here you will find a list of all your upcoming and past reservations.
-   **Cancel a Booking:** If your plans change, you can cancel a booking from the "My Bookings" page, subject to the property's cancellation policy.

## For Hosts

As a host, you can list your properties, manage your listings, and track your bookings through a dedicated dashboard.

### 1. Becoming a Host

-   **Sign Up:** When you create your account, select the "Host" role during the signup process. This will give you access to the Host Dashboard.
-   **Login:** Log in with your host account credentials to access your dashboard.

### 2. Accessing Your Dashboard

-   After logging in as a host, a "Dashboard" link will appear in the navigation bar. Clicking this will take you to your central management area.

### 3. Managing Your Properties

-   **Add a Property:** From the "Manage Properties" page in your dashboard, click "Add New Property". Fill in all the required details, including title, description, location, price, and photos.
-   **Update or Edit:** You can edit any of your property details at any time by clicking the "Edit" button next to the property in your listings table.
-   **Delete a Property:** If you no longer wish to list a property, you can remove it using the "Delete" button.

### 4. Managing Your Bookings

-   **View Bookings:** The "Bookings" section of your dashboard shows you all the upcoming and past reservations for your properties.
-   **Track Status:** Keep track of which bookings are confirmed, pending, or completed.
